Lets make some great tasting lasagna that even Garfield himself couldn't resist!

Making mums homemade special lasagna has never been easier. With a rich meaty flavour, an unbeatable cheesy filling and tomato sauce made from scratch, this traditional lasagna is to die for. You'll find no where else that does it like this. This is best served with a side of crunchy garlic bread and a top notch green salad.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/8 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Mix the ground beef and pork over medium heat in a skillet and cook/stir for 5-7 minutes then add onion and cook for approximately 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in some crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, some fresh parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce the heat to a medium-low and simmer, stir it occasionally for 30 minutes.
  3. Whilst the sauce is cooking, get a large pot of water, lightly salt it and bring to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles in the water and stir it occasionally until it is tender yet firm (around 8 minutes). Drain it and set it to the side.
  4. Whilst the noodles are cooking, preheat the oven to 190 degrees C (375 degrees F).
  5. Mix cottage cheese, parmesan cheese, eggs, teaspoon of parsley, salt and pepper in a large bowl until combined.
  6. Assemble the lasagna, use a spoon to spread the a spoonful or two of the sauce over the baking dish to coat it. Place 2 layers of noodles over the sauce and 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers then cover the dish with aluminum foil.
  7. Bake it in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake until the cheese is golden brown (5-10 minutes).
  8. Remove it from the oven and let it stand for 10 minutes before cutting into it and serving.

Hope you enjoy the tasty bake!!!
