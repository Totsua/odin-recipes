Instant Noodles

Home

Ever need a quick meal without all the hassle of cooking?

Whether you come home and are too tired to cook a full meal or you're at home and don't want to order food on a delivery app, this instant noodles meal is quick and easy without compromising the taste.

Ingredients

  • Pack of instant noodles (Personally recommended: Indomie)
  • 2 Frankfurters
  • 2 Eggs

Steps

  1. Boil the kettle with atleast 400ml of water
  2. Open the instant noodles packet and place the noodles in a bowl. Leave the seasoning it comes with on the side for now.
  3. Once the water is boiled, pour 250ml in the bowl with the noodles. Place the bowl in the microwave for 1 1/2 minutes at 800W.
  4. Whilst it heats in the microwave, heat a frying pan with some butter on high and let the butter melt. Crack the 2 eggs into the pan and let that sit.
  5. Chop up the 2 frankfurters whilst waiting for the microwave.
  6. When the microwave beeps, take the bowl out and place the packet seasonings into the noodles along with the frankfurters.
  7. Place the bowl back in the microwave for 1 minute.
  8. Check on the progress of the egg, once the egg is cooked turn off the heat and let it sit in the pan.
  9. When the microwave is finished, take out the bowl and place the eggs on top of the noodles and enjoy!

Hope you enjoy the noodles!!!
